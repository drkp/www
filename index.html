I am a researcher at Microsoft Research.

My research focus is primarily in operating systems and distributed systems.

I completed my PhD in 2017 at the University of Washington, where I was advised by Hank Levy and Arvind Krishnamurthy. My thesis is on distributed systems for applications that span mobile devices and cloud servers. Before my PhD, I received my S.B. and M.Eng. from MIT.

I was born in Beijing, China but spent most of my time growing up in Columbus, Indiana. My husband and I cook, travel and occasionally do computer science together.
